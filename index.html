Partners

W. Merritt Lancaster

Co-Founder, Principal

Merritt has been active in commercial real estate for over 20 years. As a Lender, Owner, and Developer, Merritt has learned it takes persistence, patience, knowledge, and creativity to be successful.

Merritt is responsible or over $3 billion in real estate transactions, and brings extensive knowledge of retail, office, industrial, hospitality, self-storage, multi-family and senior housing. Merritt is a Board member of Action Ministries and A+ Squash, mentoring the next generation of scholars in academic, athletic, and civic growth. Merritt resides in Inman Park with his 3 children.

Jack Arnold

Co-Founder, Principal

Jack Arnold founded Bridger Properties in 2020. His primary function is to oversee the company's day-to-day operations, as well as drive the company's strategic growth initiatives and investment activity.

A proven and trusted commercial real estate veteran and consistent top-producer, Jack brings over 16 years of experience to Bridger Properties, across a broad range of commercial real estate functions. Prior to founding Bridger, Jack spent seven years as Managing Director of Retail at Stream Realty Partners, where he founded the firm's Southeast Retail Team, building it to its position as a market leader. Jack led the Retail Team's top-producing real estate services practice, which focused on project leasing, tenant representation and property management. Additionally, Jack drove the firm's retail and mixed-use investment activity. IPrior to Stream, Jack served as Director of Retail Services at Carter and later Cassidy Turley, the successor to Carter's brokerage arm. Jack is consistently recognized as a CoStar PowerBroker for his performance.

A native Atlantan, Jack is a graduate of Washington & Lee University. He serves as a volunteer coach at Buckhead Baseball and NYO Football, and is an active member of the Peachtree Presbyterian Church. Jack currently lives in Buckhead with his wife and three children.

Strategic Investments

In addition to Bridger's primary service businesses, Bridger makes strategic investments on behalf of itself, its partners and clients. Bridger's Investments fall into two categories: programmatic and opportunistic. Bridger has institutional partners that identified Bridger as their regional investment platform, charging Bridger with executing those strategies. Bridger's opportunistic investments are the result of its unique presence in the market and are more situational in nature. Bridger invests in all asset claims and in all areas of the capital stack.
